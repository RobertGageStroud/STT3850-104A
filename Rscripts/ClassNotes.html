When creating function to model some data, explanation and prediction are potential focuses. Prediction is significantly more useful than explanation.

an estimation funciton will have a carat/hat above it. ex. y = f(x) + Error, f(x) is the signal, error is the noise.

We want to minimize the noise.

Ei = y - f(x)

wnat to minimize the sum of squares error.

f(x) = y = b_0 + b_1(X_i) b_0 - y intercept b_1 - slope

sum of squares = Sum of (y_i - b_0 - b_iX_i)^2

take the derivative of above.

set it to zero solve for whatever term was used for derivative.

it ends up being in repsect to b_0 b_0 = avg(y) - b_1*avg(x) b_1 = sum of (x_1 - avg(x))(y_i - avg(y)) / sum of (x_i - avg(x))^2

these two equations result in the minimization of the sum of square eirs.

r - correlation coefficient -1 < r < 1 perfect negative relationship will be a -1. perfect postive relationship will be 1.
